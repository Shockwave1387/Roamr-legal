Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Short version: Roamr is a free AI travel planning tool. We don't require accounts, we don't sell your data, and we don't track you for advertising. Here's exactly what we do and don't collect.

1. Who We Are

Roamr ("we", "us", "our") is a free AI-powered travel planning assistant operated by Mayfair Promotions. Available at roamrr-app.netlify.app and as a Chrome extension. We are not a travel agency, booking platform, or seller of travel. We provide information and referrals only.

2. What We Collect

Data You Provide (Optional)

  • Email & name — only if you voluntarily create an account
  • Travel search queries — destinations, dates, preferences you enter
  • Saved trips — stored in cloud only if you have an account

Data Collected Automatically

  • IP addresses — temporarily logged by hosting providers for security
  • Anonymous usage data — which features are used, to improve the app
  • Browser localStorage — stores your trips locally on your device

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment information (we never handle payments)
  • Passport or government ID details
  • Precise GPS location
  • Browsing history outside our app

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Travel prompts are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate itineraries
  • Flight searches are sent to Amadeus API for fare data
  • Weather requests use only the destination city name via OpenWeatherMap
  • Email addresses are used only for price alerts you requested and account communications

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — processes travel prompts. Privacy Policy
  • Amadeus — processes origin/destination/dates for flight search
  • OpenWeatherMap — processes destination city name for weather
  • Netlify — hosts our web app
  • Render — hosts our backend server
  • Supabase — stores account data if you create an account
  • Unsplash — serves destination photos

5. Cookies & Storage

We use localStorage to save your trips locally on your device. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or third-party analytics cookies that follow you around the web.

6. Data Deletion

  • Local data — cleared when you clear your browser cache
  • Account data — email privacy@roamr.app to delete your account
  • Server logs — automatically deleted after 30 days

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data. Email privacy@roamr.app to exercise any of these rights.

8. Children

Roamr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
