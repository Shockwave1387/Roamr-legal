How to test it
01
Open the app
Tap "Open Roamr" above. Works in any browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox. On iPhone or Android you can add it to your home screen like a native app.
02
Search a real trip
Enter your city, a destination, and dates. Try somewhere you actually want to go — Detroit to Las Vegas, Chicago to Cancun, or anywhere you've been dreaming about.
03
Click every tab
Once results load, explore all tabs across the top: Flights, Itinerary, Weather, Hotels, Best Time, Airport, Explore, Packing, Currency, Points, and Cost Breakdown.
04
Tell us everything
If something breaks, looks weird, or just doesn't feel right — that's gold for us. Your feedback directly shapes what ships when we go live. Nothing is too small to mention.
💡
About the flight prices: The prices you see during beta are AI-estimated test figures — not real bookable fares. We're testing the app's design, speed, and features right now. When we go live, flight prices will pull directly from airline systems in real time. For real current fares on any route during testing, tap "Search on Google Flights" inside the app — that always shows the real price.
